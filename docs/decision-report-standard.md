# Avalon Decision Report Standard

Every recommendation should be explainable, challengeable, and measurable.

## Thesis

What is the recommendation?

## Supporting Evidence

What evidence supports this conclusion?

## Risks

What could cause this recommendation to fail?

## Adversarial Review

What assumptions should be challenged?

## Confidence

0-100 confidence score.

## Final Recommendation

Action, No Action, or Further Research.
